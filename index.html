Who are we?

We are a "semi-company", who wants to clean up the litter that has been put everywhere, and also earn from helping the entire world!

If picking up litter had a reward, then it could be massive! Rewards make our monkey brains go bananas (pun intended)!

Rewarding litter-picking

In some nordic countries, they have introduced a concept known as: Panting! Panting is taking a material, and recycling it (turning garbage or old objects into new, usable material) and getting money, or a coupon from it!

Sweden, Norway, Finland and some other places have started making panting a thing! The most common "pant" are bottles or cans!

Every bottle or can that can be panted has a little design on it! They could vary based on the country of origin (the image below has a swedish icon).
